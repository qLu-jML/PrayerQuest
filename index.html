PrayerQuest

A gamified, offline-first prayer habit app for Android

PrayerQuest helps you build a lasting prayer rhythm. Track your prayer requests, capture gratitude, journal what God is doing, and see your streaks grow. Prayer Groups let you share requests privately with people you invite by code.

What’s inside

🔰 Eight prayer modes

Guided ACTS, voice-to-text journaling, Scripture Soak, Contemplative Silence, and more.

✨ Answered prayer catalogue

Archive answered requests with testimony, photo, or voice note — a timeline of God’s faithfulness.

🔥 Streaks & quests

XP, levels, daily quests, hearts, and achievements that keep the habit fun without feeling childish.

💪 Gratitude section

Log 1, 3, or 5 gratitudes a day, with optional photos and Scripture-themed prompts.

🧡 Prayer Groups

Invite-only groups with a shareable code. One-tap “I prayed for this” for every member.

🔒 Offline-first

Personal prayers, journals, and gratitudes stay on your device. Only Prayer Groups sync to the cloud.
